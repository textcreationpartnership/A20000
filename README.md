1. TO THE RIGHT
Honorable and righteous Iudges,
Sir THOMAS EGERTON knight,
Lord Keeper of the great Seale of England:
Sir IOHN POPHAM knight, Lord cheefe Iustice
of England: Sir EDMVND ANDERSON knight, Lord
